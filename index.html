A Way to Give Back

We here at solidarity India campaign focus on strengthening our community by being responsive to the needs that one might find difficult to meet ends with. We try to balance the economic social difference by giving our best efforts in providing resources which might not be possible because of the generic financial differences and other natural factors like COVID-19, etc. We help with food, clothes and other basic necessities. We will always strive in fulfilling the basic purpose of our existence at any cost. All profits are deemed for cause.

Reason of Our Presence

The Growing rise of COVID-19 pandemic and dismay caused because of it. A lot of people have suffered and are suffering because of this pandemic. Along with this heavy burden over our shoulders we have also been witnessing erratic floods simultaneously which has caused no less harm than any other natural calamity and was at its best talking in terms of destruction.

Our Major Causes

Covid - 19

Millions of people have suffered and are suffering because of the growing COVID-19 pandemic and thousands have been stripped off from their income sources. We strive to have their backs in every way possible.

Floods

Our country is also enduring simultaneous floods in the eastern regions which have proven to be no less than the pandemic itself.
